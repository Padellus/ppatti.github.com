About Me

For those who don't know:

Hi there.

I am Phil Patti. Currently, I'm studying Computer Science at Northwestern University in the always beautiful (as long as it's May-September) Evanston, Illinois.

Dublin, Ohio is where I grew up and where I still call home. It's a little suburb right outside of Columbus and is pretty awesome by Ohio standards.

My interests stretch all the way from general coding to web development to engine and platform development to 3D modeling to gameplay design and everywhere in between.

In my free time, I love to game (League of Legends, Guild Wars 2, Magic the Gathering, being among my favorites right now), develop games, hang with my housemates, write, sleep in on sundays, draw and sketch, watch movies, browse Reddit, play soccer, and cook & eat (my favorite foods being bacon, chocolate milk, and gravy, in that order).

-Phil
